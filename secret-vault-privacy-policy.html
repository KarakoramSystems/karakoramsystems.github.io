Privacy Policy

What Secret Vault Does

Secret Vault is a private file storage app that allows you to import and securely store photos, videos, audio files, and PDF documents on your device. The app protects access to your stored files using a PIN or Pattern lock that you set.

All files you import are managed entirely on your device — they are never uploaded to the internet or any external server.

Information We Access

a. Files You Import

When you choose to import a file into Secret Vault, the app makes a copy and stores it in the app's private local storage on your device. The original file in your gallery or file manager is not deleted or modified.

These files remain on your device at all times. We do not access, read, transmit, or upload your files to any server, cloud service, or third party. Local Only

b. PIN or Pattern Lock

The PIN or Pattern you set to protect your vault is stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted externally or accessible to us. Local Only

c. Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

Secret Vault uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Third Party

AdMob may collect certain device and usage information to serve relevant ads, such as your device's advertising ID and general usage patterns. This data collection is governed entirely by Google's Privacy Policy and is separate from any data the app itself handles. We do not directly collect, store, or process any advertising data ourselves.

How We Use Your Data

The app accesses your files and lock settings solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Storing your imported files privately in the app's local storage
  • Enforcing your PIN or Pattern lock to restrict access to the vault
  • Displaying your files using the app's built-in image viewer, video player, audio player, and PDF reader
  • Allowing you to share files directly from the vault to other apps on your device

We do not use your data for analytics, profiling, marketing, or any purpose beyond what is listed above.

Data Storage & Security

All data in Secret Vault — including your imported files and lock credentials — is stored locally in the app's private storage on your device. This storage is sandboxed by the Android operating system, meaning other apps cannot access it.

The app does not require an internet connection to function. No data is ever transmitted to Karakoram Systems or any third party by the app itself.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties, with the following exception:

  • Google AdMob — for the purpose of displaying in-app advertisements only. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles data collected through AdMob.

Permissions Used by the App

Secret Vault uses the following Android permissions:

  • RECORD_AUDIO and MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS — required by Android for audio playback functionality. They are used solely to play audio files stored in your vault. The app does not record audio, access your microphone, or capture any sound at any time.

For importing files, the app uses Android's built-in system file picker instead of requesting broad storage access. This means you explicitly choose each file you want to import — the app never has access to your entire storage or gallery.

No other sensitive permissions such as camera, contacts, or location are requested or used by the app.

Children's Privacy

Secret Vault is intended for users aged 18 and above. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

If you believe a child has used the app and you have concerns, please contact us at the email below.

Your Rights & Data Removal

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times:

  • You can delete any file from within the app
  • You can clear all app data by going to Settings › Apps › Secret Vault › Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored files and settings permanently

Because we do not collect or store your data on our servers, there is no data held by Karakoram Systems that can be requested or deleted remotely.
