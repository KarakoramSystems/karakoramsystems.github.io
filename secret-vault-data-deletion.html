Account and Data Deletion

At Karakoram Systems, we take the security of your Secret Vault seriously. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion:

Email us at: karakoramsystems@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request - Secret Vault

What data will be deleted?

Upon receiving your request, we will permanently remove:

  • Your user account and profile information.
  • Any encrypted metadata associated with your vault.
  • Cloud backup references (if applicable).

Note: Since Secret Vault is designed for privacy, once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. We process all requests within 48-72 hours.
